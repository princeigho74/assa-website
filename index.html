About ASSA

The Afiesere Secondary School Alumni Association (ASSA), Class of 2006, is a vibrant community of former students dedicated to preserving our shared heritage, supporting current students, and fostering lifelong connections among classmates.

Our Mission

To foster unity, support educational excellence, and create meaningful opportunities for our members and the school community.

Our Vision

To be a model alumni association that drives positive change and maintains lasting bonds among the Class of 2006.

Our Values

Excellence, Unity, Integrity, Service, and Pride in our Afiesere Secondary School heritage.
